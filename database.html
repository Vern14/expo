    4. Database Field
      TermOffered
      Description
      To indicate the term that the course is offered
      Legal values
      Fall, spring, fall-spring, spring-summer, summer,YearRound
      Meanings of legal values
      • Fall:The activity is only offered in the fall semester
      • Spring:The activity is only offered in the spring semester
      • Fall-spring: The activity is offered in the fall and the spring semesters
      • Spring-summer: The activity is offered in the spring and the summer terms
      • Summer: The activity is only offered in the summer
      • YearRound : The Activity is offered year round
      Type
      varchar(6)
